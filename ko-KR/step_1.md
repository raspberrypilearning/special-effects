## 자주 물어보는 질문

* To find samples available in Sonic Pi, learners can go to [jumpto.cc/sonic-pi-samples](http://jumpto.cc/sonic-pi-samples). Alternatively, they can just type `sample [space]` and choose from the list that appears.
